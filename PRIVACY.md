Labyrinth does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require an account, does not track usage, and does not include advertising.
Quote submissions entered via the in-app form are transmitted to a private Google Sheet accessible only to the developer and are used solely for the purpose of reviewing and adding quotes to the app. No personal information beyond the content of the submission itself is stored.
Notifications are scheduled locally on your device and are not processed by any external server.
